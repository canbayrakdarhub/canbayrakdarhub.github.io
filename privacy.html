FootyMinds / Ters Köşe

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information FootyMinds (known as Ters Köşe in Turkish) and the third-party services it uses collect, how that information is used, and your choices as a user.

1. Information We Collect Ourselves

FootyMinds is a local party word game. We do not run our own servers and we do not collect or store your personal information on our own infrastructure. Your game data (scores, statistics, settings, unlocked card packs, and custom cards) is stored only on your device using Apple's standard local storage (UserDefaults).

Some of the third-party services described below do, however, collect certain information automatically when you use the app — in particular to show ads and to process purchases. Details are provided in the following sections.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. To serve and measure these ads, Google and, where applicable, ad mediation and bidding partners may collect and process certain data, which can include:

  • device identifiers used for advertising (e.g. the Identifier for Advertisers / IDFA, where permitted),
  • IP address and approximate (non-precise) location derived from it,
  • device and operating-system information, and
  • ad-interaction data (impressions, clicks, and similar diagnostics).

This data is used to deliver ads, measure their performance, prevent fraud, and — subject to your consent where required — show personalized ads. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services. Where AdMob mediation is enabled, additional advertising partners (for example AppLovin) may also process advertising data under their own privacy policies.

3. Your Ad Choices & Consent

In regions where it is required (such as the EEA, UK, and Switzerland), the app shows a Google consent (UMP) prompt before serving personalized ads, and you can change your choice later. On iOS, you can also limit ad tracking through Apple's App Tracking Transparency setting and your device's privacy settings. You can remove ads entirely by purchasing the premium / Pro upgrade.

4. Purchases (Apple & RevenueCat)

In-app purchases (premium upgrade, card packs) are processed by Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage purchase entitlements and restore purchases. For this, an anonymous purchase identifier and transaction/receipt information may be processed. We do not receive your payment-card details. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Other Apple Platform Services

  • Apple Game Center – used for optional leaderboards. Governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • SharePlay (FaceTime) – used for optional multiplayer over SharePlay. No game data is stored by us during SharePlay sessions.

6. Data Retention

Data created by the app on your device (game statistics, custom card packs, preferences) lives exclusively on your device and can be deleted at any time by removing the app. Data processed by the third-party services above is retained according to their respective privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

FootyMinds is designed to be enjoyable for players of all ages, and its game content is family-friendly. Please note that the free version displays third-party advertising whose content is controlled by the ad networks and may occasionally not be suitable for younger children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided such information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
